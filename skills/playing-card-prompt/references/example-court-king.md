# Example target prompt — COURT card

A fully assembled COURT prompt (King of Spades, French deck, Anglo-American letters,
Austrian style, 9:14). Use it as a reference for the expected structure, ordering, and
spacing of a finished court-card prompt — and as a sanity check after assembly.

Note how the attributes are resolved into ONE list with no contradictions or labels:
- `full beard` (traditional) is dropped — it conflicts with the character's own
  "neat prominent mustache".
- `royal mantle / robe with ermine trim` (traditional) is replaced by the Step 5c
  "dark green military caftan with gold buttons" — only the replacement appears.
- `scepter or orb` (traditional secondary hand) is replaced by "rolled map".
- `sword held upright` (traditional primary hand) is overridden by the Step 6
  reference transfer "rapier", which also supplies the sash/medallion.
- All Step 7 exclusions are merged into one trailing `no …` list together with
  `no watermark` — nothing is repeated or labeled "exclusions".
