# Traditional JOKER attributes

Auto-loaded when the chosen rank is Joker. These fill the `[TRADITIONAL_ATTRIBUTES]`
block of the JOKER template. They are the *defaults*; the user's additions/replacements
(Step 10) and reference-image transfers (Step 11) override any of them.

```
- jester's cap with three points and hanging bells (tricorne motley)
- motley costume, divided into contrasting colored panels (traditionally red and yellow, or black and white)
- jester's bauble: short staff topped with a miniature jester's head, hung with bells
- exaggerated theatrical pose, one hand raised, light on the feet
- bells or tassels on collar and cuffs
```

Traditional hand-attribute hint string (for the "(traditional: ...)" phrasing):
- primary: jester's bauble / staff
- secondary: (open — often empty, or holding a small playing card fan)
