# Traditional KING attributes

Auto-loaded when the chosen rank is King. These fill the `[TRADITIONAL_ATTRIBUTES]`
block of the COURT template. They are the *defaults*; the user's additions/replacements
(Step 8) and reference-image transfers (Step 9) override any of them.

```
- royal crown (jeweled, closed imperial or open royal arch)
- full beard, mature commanding face
- royal mantle / robe with ermine trim
- primary hand: sword held upright
- secondary hand: scepter or orb
- enthroned, frontal regal pose, broad shoulders
```

Traditional hand-attribute hint string (for the "(traditional: ...)" phrasing):
- primary: sword
- secondary: scepter / orb
