# Traditional JACK (Knave) attributes

Auto-loaded when the chosen rank is Jack. Fill `[TRADITIONAL_ATTRIBUTES]`. Defaults;
overridden by Step 8 additions/replacements and Step 9 reference transfers.

```
- feathered cap or soft hat (NO crown)
- young clean-shaven or lightly bearded face
- page / soldier doublet with slashed sleeves
- primary hand: halberd, pike, or short sword
- secondary hand: shield, banner, or a single leaf
- alert three-quarter or profile pose
```

Traditional hand-attribute hint string:
- primary: halberd / sword
- secondary: shield / banner
